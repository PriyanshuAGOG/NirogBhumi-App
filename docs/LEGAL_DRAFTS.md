# Legal drafts — counsel review required

These are product-copy baselines, not legal advice. Publish only after review for the operating entity, jurisdiction, vendors, retention periods, grievance contact, and refund/shipping operations.

## Medical disclaimer

Nirog Bhumi is for education, lifestyle tracking, and wellness support only. It is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a doctor or qualified healthcare professional before changing medication, diet, exercise, or treatment. Seek emergency care when symptoms or readings indicate an urgent risk.

## Privacy and consent notice

The service processes identity, contact, health logs, uploaded reports, device-import data, consultations, program activity, orders, and technical diagnostics to provide requested features. Health records are not public and are shared with assigned experts only when the user consents or requests care. Nirog Bhumi does not sell health data. Optional research and marketing consent must be separate and withdrawable. Users can request access, export, correction, or deletion subject to lawful retention duties.

## Terms of use

Users must provide accurate information, protect account access, use the service lawfully, and understand that insights are educational. Staff guidance does not authorize medication changes. Misuse, fraud, harassment, unauthorized access, or attempts to compromise the service may result in suspension.

## Data deletion policy

Users may submit an in-app deletion request. Access is disabled while the verified request is processed. Data and files are deleted or irreversibly anonymized except where law, fraud prevention, payment records, or dispute handling requires limited retention. Completion status is communicated to the verified account holder.

## Refund policy

Consultation, program, and product refunds depend on cancellation timing, service delivery, payment settlement, product condition, and applicable consumer law. The checkout or booking screen must show the exact applicable terms before payment.

## Shipping policy

Availability, dispatch estimates, shipping fees, serviceable PIN codes, tracking, failed delivery, damaged goods, and return eligibility are shown at checkout and in order details. Health and hygiene products may have lawful return restrictions.

## Program terms

Programs support lifestyle consistency and do not promise cure, guaranteed reversal, medicine discontinuation, or a fixed health outcome. Outcomes vary. Participants remain responsible for medical supervision and must report concerning symptoms or readings to a qualified clinician.
